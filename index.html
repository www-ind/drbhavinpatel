Our Core Specialties

Delivering precision medical interventions through specialized clinical departments.

Clinical Gastroenterology

Expert diagnosis and management of conditions affecting the esophagus, stomach, small intestine, and colon utilizing evidence-based protocols.

Advanced Hepatology

Specialized, intensive care for acute and chronic liver diseases, ensuring optimal hepatic function and long-term metabolic health.

Therapeutic Endoscopy

State-of-the-art diagnostic and interventional endoscopic procedures performed with maximum precision and minimal patient discomfort.

Colorectal Screening & Polypectomy

Comprehensive colonoscopic screening using high-definition optics; safe, same-session polypectomy and histopathology coordination for cancer prevention.

Biliary & Pancreatic Interventions

Advanced ERCP and EUS services for stone extraction, stent placement, and minimally invasive management of pancreaticobiliary disorders.

Transplant Hepatology Consults

Multidisciplinary assessment and coordination for liver transplant candidates, including pre-transplant optimization and post-transplant care pathways.
